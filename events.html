Events & Entertainment

There's always something happening at All Stars

🎵 Live Music Fridays

Every Friday evening from 7 PM to 10 PM, enjoy live performances from local and regional musicians. From acoustic sets to full bands, our stage comes alive with talent that spans jazz, blues, rock, and pop. Grab a cocktail, settle into your seat, and let the music set the mood for an unforgettable night out.

🍸 Happy Hour

Monday through Friday, 3 PM to 6 PM. Enjoy half-price appetizers and $2 off all cocktails, draft beers, and house wines. Our happy hour is the perfect way to unwind after a long day, catch up with friends, or kick off your evening plans. No reservations needed — just walk in and enjoy the deals.

🧠 Trivia Tuesdays

Think you're the smartest person in the room? Prove it every Tuesday at 7 PM during our weekly trivia night. Teams of up to six compete across five rounds covering pop culture, history, sports, science, and a wildcard category. The winning team takes home a $50 All Stars gift card and bragging rights until next week.

🥂 Weekend Brunch

Saturday and Sunday from 10 AM to 2 PM, join us for our weekend brunch featuring bottomless mimosas, Bloody Mary bar, and a special brunch menu with favorites like eggs Benedict, French toast, and our signature All Stars breakfast burrito. A laid-back, delicious way to start your weekend.

🎤 Open Mic Wednesdays

Got talent? Every Wednesday from 7 PM, our stage is yours. Singers, comedians, poets, and musicians are welcome to sign up and perform for a supportive crowd. It's a great way to discover new local talent or showcase your own skills. Sign-up starts at 6:30 PM on a first-come, first-served basis.

🎉 Private Events

Looking for the perfect venue for your next celebration? All Stars offers private dining for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, and more. Our events team will work with you to create a customized menu and setup that fits your vision and budget. Contact us to learn more about our event packages.

Seasonal & Themed Events

Throughout the year, All Stars hosts special themed events that bring the community together. From our famous Super Bowl watch party and St. Patrick's Day celebration to summer patio parties and holiday dinners, there's always a reason to come back.

We also partner with local breweries and wineries for exclusive tasting events, giving you the chance to sample craft beverages paired with dishes designed specifically for the occasion. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay in the loop on upcoming events.

Whether you're looking for a casual night out or a special experience, All Stars delivers entertainment and hospitality that keeps our guests coming back week after week.
